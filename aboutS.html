OUR JOURNEY

Established in 1999, our journey began with a simple mission: to provide a loving home for destitute children. What started with just three residents has blossomed into Sumati Balwan, where we now nurture over 650 students learning under model of IBT(INTRODUCTION TO BASIC TECHNOLOGY)made available by SSC Board.
Our educational approach goes beyond academics, fostering emotional intelligence, creativity, and strong values. Through skill-based education and hands-on learning, we empower our students to build a sustainable future, contributing to a more eco-friendly and developed India
